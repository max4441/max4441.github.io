WEEK 3 QnA

What other media forms (digital or otherwise) does the experience reference?

The experience references physical media forms such as newspapers and journal articles. The content is laid out in a vertical format similar to reading an article in real-life. They have also added snippets of newspapers to reference this form of traditional media.

What does this reference/s communicate to you about how you should act when engaging with your research experience?

That we should slow down and take our time while reading through the contents of the website. As newspapers are usually mostly text and contain a lot of information, we should focus and digest the written information as well.

What does this reference/s communicate to you about how you should feel when engaging with your research experience?

I feel well-informed about the content of the website as it is concise and straight to the point.

What is the most frustrating part of the interaction to you and what makes that part frustrating?​​

The lack of interactivity in the website. The website could have added in more interactive elements such as a newspaper flip or playing around with the trash on the website.

What is the most satisfying part of the interaction to you and what makes that part satisfying?

The most satisfying part was how smooth the animations of the visual elements were moving as I was scrolling through the webpage. It is satisfying as it creates a seamless transition between the various content within the pages.
